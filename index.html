Welcome to CodeMallet Entertainment

CodeMallet Entertainment, Inc. is an online gaming company ran by James Chambers under the online nickname of "remote". There have been 3 projects released to date:

  • BattleCity
  • Spark
  • The Odyssey Online Classic

CodeMallet Entertainment was started to bring back online games that had been abandoned. Every one of these games I played when the original versions were out. With the exception of Odyssey, all games were written from scratch in their respective languages.

The first CodeMallet project was BattleCity which was released in February of 2005. It was written in order to learn C++ and to bring back the very first game I had ever played and my friends and I looked back on very nostalgically.

Explore the site to find links to projects that are based on CodeMallet projects. Also, if you would like to get a hold of me, check out the contact page.
